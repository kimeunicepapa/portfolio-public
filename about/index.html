About

Refined taste, a curious mind, consistent work, and a deep obsession with the craft.

Digital designer and multimedia artist, born in Italy and based in California, with a background in Design from the University of California, Davis, specializing in graphic design, UX/UI design, and web design.

My interest in design began at the intersection of art and technology, driving me to create functional and beautiful solutions.

Flexibility is essential in my creative process—it allows ideas to evolve and reach their full potential through exploration across different approaches and mediums. Adapting to unexpected outcomes, circumstances, and reactions often leads to the most fitting, impactful, and unconventional solutions.

I am committed to continuously seeking fresh perspectives, experimenting with new methods, and embracing creative exploration to push the boundaries of design.
